About Us

NATIONWIDE SUPPLY
CHAIN MASTERY

NG&J Swift Haulage & Logistics Limited is a nationwide haulage and logistics provider established in 2013. We have grown into a leading provider of third-party logistics (3PL) and supply chain solutions to businesses across Nigeria.

From retail and consumer packaged goods to automotive and beyond, our customers depend on us to drive their businesses forward through the highest standard of integrity, ingenuity, and reliable service. Our team is accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Our Foundation

VISION

& VALUES

At NG&J Swift, our purpose, direction, and principles are not aspirational — they are operational. Every shipment, every client interaction, every decision is guided by these foundations.

01
Our Vision
To act as an indispensable partner to our clients — helping them build and maximize sustainable competitive advantages by getting their products to market quickly, efficiently, and safely. Every interaction is driven by integrity and respect, the fundamental principles of our business.
02
Our Mission
To deliver a positive, reliable experience to each and every client while offering exceptional value in the marketplace and setting the standard for professionalism in logistic solutions. We operate profitably, responsibly, and with a safe and secure work environment for all.
03
Our Values
Strong relationships, agility and responsiveness, positive impact on customer success, and trust. These are not corporate slogans — they are measurable commitments we make to every client, every carrier partner, and every team member.

SHERQ & Excellence

WHY CHOOSE
NG&J SWIFT?

Our SHERQ-first approach — Safety, Health, Environment, Risk, and Quality — ensures every operation meets the highest industry standards.

Safety & Zero-Harm Culture
Every operation is planned and executed with safety as the primary consideration — for our people, your cargo, and the communities we serve.
Non-Asset Based Flexibility
As a non-asset based organization with access to a significant pool of carrier partners, we focus 100% on our customers — not infrastructure maintenance.
Sustainable Logistics
Environmental responsibility is embedded in every logistics decision — from route optimization to partner selection and packaging guidelines.
Data-Driven Risk Management
